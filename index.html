Research Project – ISRO Challenge 2025

IIITDM Kurnool (Mentorship Collaboration)

Contributed to the ISRO Innovation Challenge 2025 as part of a team working on a GPS-independent drone navigation system. The project aimed to guide drones using sensor-based data in GPS-denied environments. Under the guidance of researchers from IIITDM Kurnool, I focused on the depth estimation module to help the drone perceive distances to surrounding objects.

Key contributions:

  • Designed a depth estimation system using multi-LiDAR setup and Here Flow optical flow sensor for accurate distance sensing.
  • Evaluated different approaches to estimate distances without relying on GPS, contributing to safer drone navigation.
  • Collaborated closely with peers and mentors to integrate depth sensing with the overall navigation strategy.
